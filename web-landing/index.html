iPhone Setup

Follow these steps to install Presence on your iPhone

1
Tap "Install on iPhone" The button above will open the app in Safari. Wait for the page to load.
2
Tap Share Button Look for at the bottom of Safari.
3
Scroll & Select Scroll down in the menu and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4
Confirm Tap "Add" in the top right. Done! Find Presence on your home screen.
